# Proof-of-Work and QA SOP

Proof-of-work protects Ready White margins and brand consistency across vendors and markets.

## Required vendor uploads

- Before photos for every assigned wall.
- Same-wall 8.5 x 11 inch reference sheet visible for scope verification when applicable.
- After photos for every completed wall.
- Timestamped completion checklist.
- Notes for exceptions, access issues, color mismatch, water damage, smoke/stain blocking, severe prep, or approved change orders.

## Completion gate

A job cannot move from `Photo Proof Review` to `Completed` until an operator confirms:

1. required photos are present,
2. after photos match assigned wall IDs/scope,
3. visible coverage is acceptable,
4. exceptions are documented,
5. callback risk is low or escalated,
6. variance fields are ready for KPI reporting.

## Future AI QA layer

AI may assist with coverage checks, missed-area detection, color consistency, and wall completeness. AI does not mark jobs complete without operator-proof gates until variance and callback data justify automation.
