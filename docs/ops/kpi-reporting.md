# KPI Reporting Standards

Ready White KPI reporting should optimize cash flow, close rate, recurring revenue, automation reliability, and reduced vacancy turnover time.

## Weekly KPI Review

Track:

- New leads by source and vertical.
- Speed-to-lead median and SLA miss rate.
- Photo-complete rate.
- Photos Requested to Photos Received conversion.
- Quote turnaround time.
- Quote Sent to Approved conversion.
- Approved to Scheduled cycle time.
- Scheduled to Completed cycle time.
- Callback rate by vendor and package.
- Review request completion rate.
- Property-manager repeat lead rate.
- Closed Lost reason distribution.

## Pipeline Integrity Checks

Flag:

- Leads in **New Lead** with no first-response activity.
- Leads in **Photos Requested** with no reminder sent.
- Leads in **Quote Sent** with no follow-up.
- Approved jobs not in **Vendor Assignment**.
- Jobs in **In Progress** without photo proof.
- Completed jobs without review request.
- Opportunities with nonstandard tags or stages.
