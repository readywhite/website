# Estimating Context

## Objective
Use AI-assisted estimating and photo-based scope verification to accelerate quotes while reducing scope drift, margin leakage, and vendor disputes.

## Customer Photo Requirements
Require every customer to submit:
- 1 wide photo of each room.
- 1 photo of the worst wall in each room.
- Photos of ceilings, trim, stains, peeling paint, water damage, smoke damage, holes, and heavy prep areas.

## Photo Review Goals
Use submitted photos to:
- Confirm room count and room type.
- Identify standard package fit.
- Detect exception jobs.
- Prevent scope drift.
- Protect margins.
- Maintain quote speed.
- Prepare vendor-ready scopes.

## AI-Assisted Estimating Flow
1. Intake lead from Squarespace or GHL.
2. Request required photo set.
3. Verify required photo coverage.
4. Classify room and condition bands.
5. Flag prep, ceiling, trim, stain, smoke, water, or repair exceptions.
6. Map scope to package pricing and add-ons.
7. Route edge cases to human review.
8. Send quote through the approved GHL pipeline stage.

## Exception Controls
Escalate instead of auto-quoting when photos indicate:
- Water damage.
- Smoke damage.
- Heavy staining.
- Peeling paint.
- Large holes.
- Unknown substrate issues.
- Unclear room coverage.
- Missing worst-wall photos.
