# Ready White Photo Intake Policy

## Required customer photos

Every customer photo request should require:

- 1 wide photo of each room.
- 1 photo of the worst wall in each room.
- Photos of ceilings.
- Photos of trim.
- Photos of stains.
- Photos of peeling paint.
- Photos of water damage.
- Photos of smoke damage.
- Photos of holes.
- Photos of heavy prep areas.

## Operational purpose

These photos are required to:

- prevent scope drift;
- protect margins;
- detect exception jobs;
- preserve quote speed;
- improve vendor assignment accuracy;
- reduce vacancy turnover time.

## Exception escalation

Escalate to exception review when photos show:

- severe prep;
- water damage;
- smoke or stain blocking;
- custom repairs;
- holes or damaged drywall;
- peeling paint across multiple rooms;
- unclear scope that threatens package standardization.
