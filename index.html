FAQ

Standardized does not mean unclear.

Is Ready White a traditional painter?

No. Ready White is positioned as a standardized property refresh management company using package lanes, photo review, and subcontractor-led fulfillment.

Do I still get a quote?

Yes. The difference is that the quote starts from a standardized package architecture, then exceptions are verified instead of beginning with a fully custom estimate process.

Can you handle heavy turns?

Yes. Heavy Turn Reset is the exception lane for damage-heavy properties and requires scope verification before scheduling.

Does this integrate with GHL?

Yes. The secure backend route supports contact upsert and optional opportunity creation using Railway environment variables.
