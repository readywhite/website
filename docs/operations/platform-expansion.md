# Platform Expansion Strategy

Ready White is a logistics and operations company that happens to perform repaint services first. Repaint is the wedge; the durable platform is dispatch infrastructure, QA systems, vendor intelligence, property-turnover workflows, and market pricing controls.

## Expansion rule

Do not add a new service line until repaint workflows prove repeatable in at least one market with stable intake, pricing, fulfillment, QA, variance tracking, and vendor scoring.

## Future service candidates

- cleaning,
- flooring,
- punch work,
- handyman repairs,
- inspections,
- light maintenance,
- full make-ready turns.

## Required controls before adding a service

1. Standard intake object.
2. Package pricing rules and exception flags.
3. Vendor scorecard fields.
4. Proof-of-work checklist.
5. QA sampling rules.
6. Variance metrics.
7. GHL tags/stages or sub-workflow mapping.
8. Market multiplier assumptions.
9. Stale-work recovery flow.
10. Control-system thresholds.

## Strategic identity

Ready White should continue to operate as vendor-orchestrated field-service infrastructure: routing the right job to the right vendor at the right margin with the right confidence and the right proof gates.
