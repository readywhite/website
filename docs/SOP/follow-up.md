# SOP: Follow-Up

## Goal

Win more jobs by following up consistently until the lead approves, declines, or becomes unqualified.

## Owner

SDR

## Trigger

Opportunity enters **Quote Sent** or **Follow-Up**.

## Sequence

- Day 1: SMS and call.
- Day 3: SMS and email.
- Day 7: call, SMS, and voicemail if no answer.
- Day 14: final follow-up and close-lost review if no response.

## Steps

1. Complete all due follow-up tasks daily.
2. Log every touch in GHL notes.
3. Move interested leads to **Approved** when they approve.
4. Escalate high-intent or stalled leads to COO.
5. Move unresponsive leads to **Closed Lost** only with a lost reason.

## Done Definition

- No overdue follow-up tasks remain at end of day.
- Every quote has a follow-up trail.
- Closed Lost opportunities include a reason.
