# SOP: Lead Intake

## Goal

Turn every new Ready White lead into a clean GHL opportunity with a fast first touch and a clear next step.

## Owner

SDR

## Trigger

A new lead enters **Ready White Customer Jobs → New Lead**.

## Steps

1. Call the lead within 5 minutes during working hours.
2. If no answer, leave the approved voicemail and send the new-lead SMS.
3. Confirm name, phone, email, property address, service needed, timeline, and vacancy status.
4. Confirm whether photos were submitted.
5. If photos are missing, move to **Photos Requested** and send the photo request SMS.
6. If photos are present, move to **Photos Received** and create/confirm COO quote-review task.
7. Add a note summarizing the contact attempt and next step.
8. Make sure the opportunity has an owner and next task.

## Done Definition

- Lead has been contacted or attempted.
- Opportunity stage is accurate.
- Next task exists.
- Notes explain the current status.
