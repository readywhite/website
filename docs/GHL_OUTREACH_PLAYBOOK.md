# Ready White GHL Outreach Playbook

Use these scripts in GoHighLevel templates, snippets, and workflows. Keep messages short, specific, and action-oriented.

## Auto-Reply SMS: New Lead

```text
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, thanks for contacting Ready White.

We received your property refresh request and will review your details/photos shortly.

If you have more photos, reply here and send them over.

- Ready White
```

## Auto-Reply Email: New Lead

**Subject:** We Received Your Ready White Request

```text
Hi {{contact.first_name}},

Thanks for reaching out to Ready White.

We received your property refresh request. Our team will review the property details, notes, and photos so we can move quickly on the next step.

If you have additional photos of the rooms, walls, ceilings, trim, or problem areas, reply to this email with them attached.

- Ready White
```

## SDR Call Opener

```text
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, this is {{user.first_name}} with Ready White. I saw your property refresh request come in and wanted to quickly confirm a few details so we can get you moving.

Is now a bad time for 60 seconds?
```

## SDR Voicemail

```text
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, this is {{user.first_name}} with Ready White. We received your property refresh painting request. I’m calling to confirm the property details and make sure we have the right photos for a fast quote. You can call or text me back at this number. Thanks.
```

## Missing Photos SMS

```text
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, Ready White can move faster if we have photos of the rooms/walls that need painting.

Please reply with photos when you can. Wide room shots plus close-ups of damaged areas are perfect.

- Ready White
```

## Missing Details SMS

```text
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, quick question so we can review your painting request:

Is the property vacant, occupied, or between tenants right now?
```

## Quote Sent SMS

```text
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, Ready White sent your property refresh quote.

Take a look when you can. If you want to approve it or have questions, reply here and we’ll help.
```

## Follow-Up Day 2 SMS

```text
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, just checking in on the Ready White quote for {{contact.property_address}}.

Do you want us to help get this scheduled?
```

## Follow-Up Day 4 Email

**Subject:** Still need help with the property refresh?

```text
Hi {{contact.first_name}},

Just following up on the Ready White painting quote.

If you still need the property refreshed, reply with any questions or timing updates and we can help you move it forward.

- Ready White
```

## Approved Next Step SMS

```text
Thanks {{contact.first_name}} — we have your approval.

Ready White will confirm scheduling details next and keep you updated before work begins.
```

## Completed Job Review SMS

```text
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, thanks for choosing Ready White.

If everything looks good, would you be open to leaving us a quick review or sending us the next property that needs a refresh?
```

## SDR Daily Workflow

1. Start with New Lead and Photos Requested stages.
2. Call every untouched lead first.
3. Text every no-answer lead immediately after voicemail.
4. Move missing-photo leads to Photos Requested.
5. Move responsive/photo-ready leads to Photos Received.
6. Log every touch in notes.
7. End the day with no overdue tasks.

## COO Daily Workflow

1. Review Photos Received and Quote Sent first.
2. Clear quote bottlenecks.
3. Check Quote Sent and Follow-Up for stale opportunities.
4. Review Approved and Scheduled for operational handoff.
5. Confirm Completed jobs have notes, photos, and next action.
