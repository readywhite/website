# Ready White North Star

## Mission

Ready White exists to make vacant and turnover properties rent-ready within days, not weeks, through fast photo-based painting quotes, disciplined follow-up, and clean operational execution.

## Customer

Primary customers:

- property managers
- real estate investors
- landlords
- realtors
- apartment operators
- commercial owners with fast refresh needs

Best-fit jobs:

- vacant turnover painting
- rental repainting
- investor refreshes
- move-out painting
- wall, ceiling, and trim repainting
- heavy reset projects that need speed and clarity

## Primary KPI

**Time from lead submission to scheduled job.**

Every system, script, workflow, VA task, and Codex improvement should reduce this time while protecting job quality and margin.

## Success Metrics

- Lead response under 5 minutes during working hours.
- Quote path started within 30 minutes of receiving enough details/photos.
- Quote sent within 24 hours of complete scope/photos.
- Job scheduled within 48 hours after approval.
- Every open opportunity has an owner, stage, next task, and latest note.
- No open opportunity sits untouched for more than 24 business hours.
- Lost leads have a clear lost reason.
- Completed jobs have closeout notes and photos when available.

## Speed Standards

| Event | Standard | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- |
| New lead received | First call/SMS attempt within 5 minutes | SDR |
| No answer | Voicemail + SMS immediately | SDR |
| Photos missing | Photo request within 10 minutes | SDR |
| Photos received | Quote review task same day | COO |
| Quote sent | Follow-up task created immediately | SDR |
| Quote approved | Scheduling task same day | COO |
| Job completed | Closeout notes same day | COO |

## Operational Rules

1. If it is not in GoHighLevel, it does not exist operationally.
2. Every lead must have a pipeline stage, owner, next task, and latest note.
3. The SDR owns speed-to-lead and follow-up consistency.
4. The COO owns pipeline hygiene, scheduling, QA, and missed-follow-up recovery.
5. Squarespace stays visual and customer-facing.
6. Railway stays private, secure, and integration-focused.
7. GoHighLevel stays the CRM, automation, task, and pipeline system of record.
8. Codex changes must preserve naming standards and deterministic commands.
9. Automate only after the manual operating rule is clear.
10. Optimize for scheduled jobs, not vanity activity.

## What Winning Means

Ready White is winning when:

- property teams get a fast response and clear next step
- photos/details move quickly into quote review
- every qualified lead is followed up until won or intentionally closed
- COO and SDR know exactly what to do every day
- GHL shows the truth of the business in real time
- Railway quietly handles secure integrations without exposing secrets
- Codex can audit, report, and improve the system without re-learning the business
