# Ready White Vendor Policy

Ready White should not build vendor fulfillment around flat hourly labor.

## Standard vendor structure

Use:

- preset package buy rates;
- standard size bands;
- standard add-ons;
- exception escalation workflows.

## Hourly/change-order limits

Hourly or change-order rates are only for:

- severe prep;
- water damage;
- smoke/stain blocking;
- custom repairs;
- approved scope exceptions.

## Required vendor controls

Always enforce:

- vendor scorecards;
- response SLAs;
- photo proof requirements;
- standardized scopes;
- callback tracking;
- operational redundancy.
